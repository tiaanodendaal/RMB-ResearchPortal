Theuns joined RMB in 2007 as a Fixed Income Strategist in the RMB Global Markets Research team. In 2008 he moved to FirstRand's Macro Portfolio Management team where he was responsible for macroeconomic analysis and strategy. He was appointed Head of RMB Global Markets Research in April 2010.

Before joining the FirstRand Group, he served as an Emerging Markets Analyst and later as a Fixed Income Strategist in the financial sector from 2001 to 2007. Theuns received top ratings in the Financial Mail rankings in the Fixed Income category and was also Best Fixed Income Analyst at the JSE Spire Awards in 2006 and 2007. He started his career as a lecturer in the Department of Economics at the University of Pretoria in the late 1990s.

Theuns received a MCom in Econometrics from the University of Pretoria before completing an MA in Economics at the University of Toronto. He was awarded his PhD in Economics from the University of Pretoria in 2004.
